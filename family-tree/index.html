📖 Instructions & Navigation

🖱️ Mouse Controls:

  • Click and drag on empty space to pan around
  • Click on any person to view their details
  • Use zoom buttons or scroll wheel with Ctrl/Cmd
  • Double-click empty space to reset view

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • Arrow Keys: Pan in any direction
  • + / =: Zoom in
  • - : Zoom out
  • 0: Reset to default view
  • F: Auto-fit entire tree

✨ Features:

  • Each spouse gets their own separate box with smooth curved connections
  • Clean, modern design with gradient colors and smooth animations
  • Automatic optimal spacing and alignment of generations
  • Load your own data from Google Sheets (CSV format)
  • Required columns: ID, Name, generation, SpouseID, Parent1ID, Parent2ID, BirthYear (optional)
